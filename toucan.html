About Golden Toucan

Golden Eggs is a DeFi Token hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource. It is the first Defi Dapp that is easy to use, thanks to the "One Click Staking" function, and safe thanks to it's embedded secured-liquidity mechanism.

When a player invest Trx to get the Golden Toucan, the smartcontract gives 90% of the investment to the previous Toucan owner, 5% to marketing fund, and 5% to GLDI liquidity in justswap: one half is used to buy GLDI token, the other half is used to provide liquidity to the GLDI/TRX Pool, among with the just bought GLDI. The resulting LP (liquidity pool) tokens are then locked forever. This offers a great protection against impermanent losses also known as "rug pull", since all liquidity is always locked and nobody can take advantage of the system.

Every time the toucan changes owner, its price increases of +15%.
If no purchases, its price decrease of -1.5% everyday.

GoldenEggs is an entertainment vehicle and not an investment product. 
Claimed Eggs are instantly minted by the official smartcontract, +10% is minted to marketing fund.
