  • What is GoldenEggs.finance? 

    Golden Eggs is a DeFi ecosystem composed of two Defi Tokens and many Farming contracts, hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource.

  • How it Works? How to Interact?

    GoldenEggs project is simple:
    - Users acquire shares of a Farm (Animals/Creatures), by investing either TRX or GLDI/GLDE tokens. 
    - These animals/creatures produces an amount of tokens (GLDI/GLDE) and distributes them among farmers, every day, forever!
    - Farmers are free to claim their produced tokens (rewards) at any time, and either hold them or sell them for TRX or reinvest them in magical farms.

  • What does Chickens do?

    Golden Chickens produces Golden Eggs (GLDE) continuously, and distributes them among all Chickens holders.
    By holding Chickens, you will get Golden Eggs (GLDE) everyday, forever. 
    When a player get Chickens, half his TRX are used to buy GLDE from Justswap, the other half are used to add liquidity (among with the just bought GLDE) to the GLDE/TRX exchange liquidity pool.
    This gives great support and value to Golden Eggs (GLDE) token.

  • How is the value of Chickens, Gooses, Bunnies, Gnomes, Dragons and Fairies calculated?

    Chickens value is same as GLDE/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDE pool in justswap).
    Gooses value is same as GLDI/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDI pool in justswap).
    Bunnies value is same as GLDE/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDE pool in justswap).
    Flamingos value is same as GLDI/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDI pool in justswap).
    Gnomes value is same as GLDE value (1 Gnome = 1 GLDE).
    Dragons value is same as GLDE + GLDI value (1 Dragon = 1 GLDE + 1 GLDI).
    Fairies value is same as GLDI value (1 Fairy = 1 GLDI).

  • What is the difference between Animals Farms and Magical Farms?

    Animals Farms (Chickens, Gooses, Bunnies, Flamingos, Penguins) are acquired by spending TRX, those TRX are injected into the GLDE and GLDI liquidity pools. These liquidity pool  release a special kind of token (Liquidity Tokens), that are the Animals. Thus, Animals value are tied to liquidity. Since liquidity in Goldeneggs Dapp is locked by default, Animals value tend to remain quite stable.
    Magical Farms (Gnomes, Fairies, Dragons) are acquired by spending Tokens (GLDE or GLDI), those Tokens are locked forever in the farm contract (burned). Thus, Magical Entities (Gnomes, Fairies, Dragons) value is tied to the Tokens value. Since GLDE and GLDI price depend exclusively from supply and demand (how the community interact with the tokens), their value tend to be more volatile and much less stable compared to Animals (LP tokens) value.

  • What do Golden Peacock and Golden Toucan do?

    Golden Peacock and Golden Toucan are the NFT (non functional token) of GoldenEggs ecosystem!
    There is only ONE Peacock and ONE Toucan, and they can only have one owner per time.
    Anytime the Peacock or Toucan is bought, 90% of trx spent by the new owner goes to refund the previous owner! The rest of trx are used for provide liquidity to GLDI or GLDE.
    Anytime a new owner buy the Peacock or Toucan, its price increase of +15%!
    Everyday the price decrease of -1.5%.
    Peacock and Toucan produces GLDI and GLDE every day for their owners.

  • Governance and "Farmocracy"

    GoldenEggs is a community oriented project, every evolutionary step made in development (examples: create a new farm, increase/decrease tokens production of farms, create a new mini-game, etc) is voted and decided by community. "Farmocracy" (farmers democracy) consist on the community voting act, on the official goldeneggs telegram group, where from time to time users are called to vote (polls) on future directions of the ecosystem.

  • How can I make profit and how much?

    In every Farm page, you can see detailed real-time stats such as Daily ROI%, APR (yearly profit %), etc. You can sell your GLDE and GLDI in the Golden DEX, or in Justswap  by clicking the buttons "TRADE" in the main page for each token, and easily convert them for TRX, USDT, or any other asset you can think of.

  • Free Energy

    Every transaction in the Tron blockchain consumes "energy" (please visit the official Tron websites to learn more on how Tron cryptocurrency works), although energy costs are extremely cheap (compared to other cryptos), GoldenEggs ecosystem have two energy community-pools to help users in getting free energy when interacting with farms smartcontracts, one pool is dedicated to Penguins farm (that aim to be 100% energy-free for its farmers), the other pool is shared among all other farms (since there are many users and many farms, not everybody might get free transactions all the time, but still it's a big help).

  • What about the value of GLDI and GLDE?

    Golden Eggs (GLDE) and Golden Diamonds (GLDI) tokens are the official GoldenEggs Ecosystem utility tokens.
    They are inflationary by nature, every day new tokens are produced in the farms and given to farmers, forever. The supply is potentially unlimited, and the daily produced amount can vary from time to time (farmocracy).
    The main use case (utility) of GLDI and GLDE tokens is to allow farmers to generate profits by claiming (rewards) them and converting them to other assets (trx, usdt, btc, eth, etc.), or reinvesting them (compound) in order to gain even  bigger profits in the future. 
    GLDI and GLDE value is completely decentralized and governed by supply and demand forces, their prices go up whenever they are bought, and go down whenever they are sold.

About Golden Eggs

Golden Eggs is a DeFi ecosystem composed of two Defi Tokens and many Farming contracts, hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource.

It is the first Defi Dapp that is easy to use, thanks to the "One Click Staking" function, and safe thanks to it's embedded secured-liquidity mechanism. 
All liquidity is always locked forever, by default. This offers a great protection against impermanent losses also known as "rug pull", since nobody can take advantage of the system.

Golden Eggs tokens price is totally dictated by supply and demand dynamics, the whole dapp is fully decentralized all the time. Golden Eggs tokens can be bought and sold at any time, and they can be also used to farm additional tokens (magical farms).

Funds allocation (Animal farms): 5% to marketing, 5% to referral (referral program), 90% to staking.
Claimed tokens are instantly minted by the official smartcontracts, +10% is minted to marketing fund.

GoldenEggs is an entertainment vehicle and not an investment product.
