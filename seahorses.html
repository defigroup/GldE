About Golden Seahorses

Golden Seahorses is the only unlocked farm of GoldenEggs ecosystem!

Stake GLDS to get seahorses (1 GLDS = 1 Seahorse).

Seahorses farm do not last forever, it only last for 30 days.
GoldenShells cannot be "minted", thus the duration of Seahorses farm is limited.

Farmers are free to stake (get Seahorses) and unstake their GLDS at any time

Seahorses have 0 fees, however GLDS tokens still have the deflationary function on every transfer (5% burned).
