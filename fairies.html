About Golden Fairies

Golden Eggs and Golden Diamonds are DeFi Tokens hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource. It is the first Defi Dapp that is easy to use, thanks to the "One Click Staking" function, and safe thanks to it's embedded secured-liquidity mechanism.
Golden Diamonds and Golden Eggs are produced by Golden Fairies! Golden Fairies are created by offering Golden Diamonds, Offered Golden Diamonds (GLDI) are sent to the Farm smartcontract and locked forever, there is no unstake function at all. This means Golden Fairies will keep producing Golden Diamonds and Golden Eggs forever.
This offers a great protection against impermanent losses also known as "rug pull", since all liquidity is always locked and nobody can take advantage of the system.
Golden Eggs and Golden Diamonds price is totally dictated by supply and demand dynamics, the whole dapp is fully decentralized all the time.
Claimed Diamonds and Eggs are instantly minted by the official smartcontract, +10% is minted to marketing fund.
